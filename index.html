Professional trip planning for guides and adventure groups

Whether you're running a guiding business or organizing group adventures, stop juggling spreadsheets, email threads, and group chats. AdventureForge brings all your trip planning, client communication, and logistics into one professional platform.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Plan your route with detailed daily breakdowns, waypoints, and timing estimates.

Attendee Management

Track RSVPs, invite participants, and designate emergency contacts with clear status indicators.

Meal Planning

Organize meals for the entire trip, assign responsibilities, and generate shopping lists with ingredient aggregation.

Transport Coordination

Arrange carpools, track who's driving, and ensure everyone gets to the trailhead.

Gear & Packing Lists

Create shared group gear lists and personal packing checklists. Use templates for common trips.

Cost Splitting

Track expenses, split costs fairly, and see who owes what with automatic settlement calculations.

Route Mapping

Upload GPX files, visualize your route on interactive maps, and share coordinates with the group.

Group Discussion

Keep all trip communication in one place with real-time messaging and file sharing.

Task Management

Assign pre-trip tasks, track progress, and ensure nothing gets forgotten before departure.
